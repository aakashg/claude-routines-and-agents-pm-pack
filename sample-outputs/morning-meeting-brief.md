## What's good about this output

- **Specific open asks named.** "You haven't replied to the cohort-2 question" — not "be prepared to discuss pricing."
- **One actionable line per meeting.** "Come with a recommendation, not options." That's a PM being served intel.
- **Nothing is filler.** No "this should be a great discussion."

## What to expect on day 1

Your first brief will feel shorter and more generic than this. Two reasons:

1. The agent hasn't yet seen enough thread history to weight context.
2. Your prompt probably wasn't specific enough about format. Tune after 3-4 runs.
